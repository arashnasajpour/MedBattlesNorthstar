About MedBattles

<<<<<<< HEAD

MedBattles is an AI-powered medical card game and simulation platform designed to enhance learning for medical students, doctors, and educators. By combining gamification and AI-driven problem-solving, MedBattles helps users explore real-world scenarios through dynamic case-based learning.

=======

Why MedBattles?

MedBattles introduces an interactive learning experience through AI-driven clinical scenarios that simulate real-world patient cases.

Our Mission

To enhance medical education by gamifying clinical training with cutting-edge AI-powered feedback.
